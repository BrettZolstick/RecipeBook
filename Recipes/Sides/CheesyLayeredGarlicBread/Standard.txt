
Cheesy Layered Garlic Bread

    Source - The Apron
	https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I0YD30ZUjnI&list=PLkjEwiIaIg4L2w3sQvh_dEIgZkhFKjAX7&index=8





# Ingredients - Serves 4

	# Bread

		225ml 	- Milk
		30g 	- Sugar
		4.5g 	- Instant Dry Yeast
		300g 	- Bread Flour
		5g 		- Salt
		20g 	- Soft Unsalted Butter

	# Garlic Sauce

		2tbsp	- Garlic Paste
		50g 	- Soft Unsalted Butter
		1tsp 	- Chilli Flakes
		150g 	- Mozzarella Cheese
		100g 	- Chedder Cheese
				- Salt to taste
				- Chopped Parsley



				
# Cookware

	2 Mixing bowls (one larger one smaller)
	Seive 
	Bread Mold
	Whisk
	Parchment paper
	



# Instructions

	1. Yeast mixture
		1. Measure 225ml of room temperature milk (temperature is important to not kill the yeast)
		2. Add 30g of sugar
		3. Add 4.5g of Instant Dry Yeast

	2. Dough
		1. Sift 300g of bread flower into a mixing bowl
		2. Add 5g of salt (Mix well before adding wet ingredients)
		3. Add the yeast mixture
		4. Knead by hand for 3-4 minutes
		5. (Optional) Move to a mixer
		6. Add 20g of soft unsalted butter.
		7. Knead until dough passes the windowpane test (can stretch it into a translucent window)

	3. Proofing
		1. Place the dough in a greased container and cover.
		2. Let it rise for 1 hour (best if done in a turned off oven/microwave with a cup of hot water for humidity)

	4. Garlic Butter
		1. Mix the following
		 	2tbsp	- Garlic Paste
 	 		50g 	- Soft Unsalted Butter
 	 		1tsp 	- Chilli Flakes
 	 				- Salt to taste
 	 				- Chopped Parsley


 	5. Shaping
 	 	1. Punch down the dough to release the air.
 	 	2. Roll onto a floured table, so that it becomes a flat rectangle
 	 	3. Spread the garlic butter all over the surface
 	 	4. Add a layer of mozzerella and chedder cheeze
 	 	5. Cut, stack, and arrange in a bread mold

	6. Second Proofing
		1. Let the dough rise for another hour

	# Remeber to preheat your oven to 350 F (180 C) before you're ready to start baking.

	7. Baking
		1. Bake for 30 minutes in a 350 F  (180 C) oven. Until the bread is golden brown and crispy.

	8. Serving 
		1. Remove from bread mold and serve on any dish or bread basket and enjoy.
